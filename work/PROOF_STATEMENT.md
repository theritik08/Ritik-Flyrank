# Proof Statement

I can rigorously validate ML models — catching my own leakage, bugs, 
and false-positive results before they reach a decision-maker — a 
skill I want an ML team lead evaluating candidates beyond inflated 
resume metrics to see, so they open my capstone repo and judge the 
process, not just the number.

## Why This Needs to Exist

A CV or LinkedIn can list "AUC 0.84," but it can't show that I caught 
432 false-positive opportunities in my own model and rejected XGBoost 
when it quietly overfit — this repo is the only place that process 
is visible.
